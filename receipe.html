Sheet Pan Steak

  • Prep Time:10 mins
  • Cook Time:1 hr 6 min
  • Marinate Time:30 mins
  • Total Time:1 hr 46 mins
  • Servings:4

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound tenderized round steak
  2. 1 (1.06 ounce) package Brazilian steakhouse marinade mix, such as McCormick® Grill Mates® Brazilian Steakhouse Marinade
  3. 1/4 cup oil 1/4 cup water
  4. 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar 3 cups baby potatoes, halved
  5. 1 pound French green beans, trimmed 1 (1.25 ounce) package toasted onion & garlic potato seasoning, such as McCormick® Toasted Onion & Garlic Potato Seasoning 2 tablespoons oil

Directions

Step 1

Steak in a resealable plastic bag. Whisk together marinade mix, 1/4 cup oil, water, and red wine vinegar in a small bowl; pour into the bag. Reseal the bag, pressing out excess air, and squeeze bag to coat steak. Marinate steak in the refrigerator for a minimum of 30 minutes or up to overnight.

Step 2

Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with foil.

Step 3

Place baby potatoes, green beans, and 2 tablespoons oil in a large resealable plastic bag; toss well to coat. Sprinkle in potato seasoning, reseal the bag, and toss again.

Step 4

Spread vegetables in a single layer on the prepared sheet; add steak on top of vegetables. Bake in the preheated oven until potatoes are tender and steaks are firm and reddish-pink and juicy in the center, about 1 hour. Turn on the broiler, and broil steak for 3 minutes. Turn steak over and broil 3 minutes more. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 130 degrees F (54 degrees C). Serve immediately.

Chikenkarai

Chicken Karahi is a tomato and ginger based, thick masala curry which is though to originate from the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa region. The original, authentic method of making it is a very specific one. As a general rule, Karahis are made from a base of ginger, garlic and tomatoes and also contain fresh green chillis, julienne-cut raw ginger and coriander. It’s a rather thick, jammy gravy and concentrated in flavour – yum!

Ingredients

  1. Meat
  2. Fresh Garlic
  3. Fresh Ginger Fresh Tomatoes
  4. Spices Green Chilli+ Coriander

HOW TO MAKE KARAHI

    Ingredients

  1. 1 kg Beef 4 adad Darmiany size k pyaz 2 tbsp Lehson adrak ka paste


  2. 1 inch ka tukra adrak ka 1/2 cup dahi 3 tbsp khule dodh ki balai


  3. 4 adad tmater 1 tsp Kutti lal mirch 1 tsp Kutti kali mirch


  4. 4 adad Sabat green chilli moti wali 2 tbsp Dhania powder 2 tbsp zeera powder


  5. to taste Salt to taste Lal mirch powder 1/2 cup oil/ghee(ziada b dal skty hn)


  6. Haldi thori se 1 tsp dhania kata hua garnish k lye

Cooking Instructions

Step 1

Cooker mein oil dalain ab is me pyaz ko halka sa soty krain brown ni krna ab is me lehson adrak ka paste dalain or thora sa pkain. Phr is me ghost, namak or haldi dal kr 2 glass pani dalain or 10 mint k lye cooker chala dain. 10 mint k bad cooker khol kr check krain 70 percent ghost ko cooker me galana chahye.

Step 2

Ghost gall jany pr usy karai me transfer krain (baki 30 percent ghost karai me galana hai) or tmater dal kr achi trah bhonain. Ab is me kutti lal mirch, kutti kali mirch, zeera powder, dhania powder, lal mirch powder or adrak ka tukra barik kat kr shamil krain. Ab isy bhonain.

Step 3

Green chilli ko aik aik cut lga kr dal dain. Ab dahi me balai dal kr mix krain. Anch halki krain or dahi balai, karai me shamil kr dain. Aur mix krain. Ab low flame pr 3 mint k lye dhak k pkain.

Step 4

Jab oil uper aa jai to dish out krain (dhania or barik katti adrak sy garnish krain).
